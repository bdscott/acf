Your toaster is talking! a 2-day camp for inquisitive kids

Interesting things happen when the real world meets the power of the Internet.

Over the course of two full days, your student will learn the foundations of engineering and robotics from experts in the field.

No previous coding experience is necessary, and students will get their hands dirty on day one, building real projects with help from our instructors.


The Details

Grades 3-8
July 7th & 8th, 10am-4pm
The New School in Fayetteville, AR


Students from all schools (or home schools) are welcome.

Lunch provided, vegan and GF available.

All necessary computers and hardware provided.


Camp registration is $125 for students who participated in the Arkansas Code Festival. This rate (a $25 discount) is valid through Friday, May 22nd when general registration opens.
